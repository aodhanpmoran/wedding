Church

The Sacrament of Marriage will be celebrated on November 8, 2025, at 1PM in St. Mary's Catholic Church, Claddagh Quay, Galway, Ireland. Please arrive by 12:30PM to be seated before we begin.

Hotel

After the ceremony, shuttle buses will be provided to transport everyone from the church to The Hardiman hotel. For those who wish to walk, it is a 10 minute walk from the church to the hotel through the beautiful Latin Quarter.

The drinks reception will start in the hotel lobby at 2:30PM, followed by dinner and celebration at the same venue.

Airports

The closest airports are Ireland West Airport Knock (87km, about 1 hour) and Shannon Airport (85km, about 1 hour) from Galway. Dublin Airport (212km, about 2 hours 20 minutes) is also an option with more international flights.

Taxis, buses, and car rentals are readily available from all airports. For Dublin Airport, you can book direct buses to Galway city with Citylink.

Accommodation

We've reserved a block of rooms at The Hardiman, which is also our reception venue, making it incredibly convenient. You can call +353 91 564 041 or book online.

There are also plenty of lovely guesthouses, Airbnbs, and hotels nearby in Galway city center.
